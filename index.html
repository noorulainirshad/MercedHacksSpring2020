Glean

About Us

Glean allows family farms and gardening hobbyists to supply their extra
produce to people in need.This way, less food goes to waste. A great way
to get to know your community, and a great way to help friends and families
in need.

Donors

Anybody can be a donor! Glean helps organize events where people can
meet up to trade foods they do not need. This will give people the opportunity
to give others resources that would have gone to waste otherwise
